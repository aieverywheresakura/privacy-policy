Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application “LiveTranslator”, developed and published by Beijing Zilolan Technology Co., Ltd.

1. Information Collection

The App requires access to the device microphone to capture audio input for real-time speech recognition and translation.

The App does not collect or store personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or account credentials.

2. Use of Information

Audio and text data are processed solely to:

  • Perform real-time speech recognition
  • Translate spoken content between languages

3. Data Processing and Storage

Audio and text data may be temporarily transmitted to third-party speech recognition or translation services to enable core functionality.

No audio or translated text is permanently stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party APIs (such as cloud-based speech recognition or translation services). These services process data only as necessary to provide the requested functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. User Rights

Users may stop using the App at any time. Since no personal data is stored, no data deletion request is required.
