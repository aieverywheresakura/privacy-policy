Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application “LiveTranslator” (the “App”), developed and published by Beijing Zilolan Technology Co., Ltd. (“we”, “us”, “our”).

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Account authentication tokens (for maintaining your login session)

We use this information to provide account functionality (sign-in, account security) and to associate your subscription status with your account.

1.2 Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes the payment and manages subscription status via your Apple ID. We may receive non-payment information related to your subscription status (e.g., whether you are subscribed) to unlock premium features.

We do not collect or store your full payment details (such as credit card numbers).

1.3 Speech & Text Data (Core Functionality)

LiveTranslator requires access to the device microphone to provide real-time speech recognition and translation. Depending on your settings and selected mode, the App processes audio and/or text to deliver translations.

  • Microphone audio may be processed for speech recognition.
  • Recognized text may be processed for translation.

We do not permanently store audio recordings, speech transcripts, or translation content on our servers.

1.4 Usage & Diagnostics Data

We may collect limited, non-content technical information to improve stability and performance, such as:

  • Crash logs
  • Performance metrics (e.g., latency, errors)
  • Device and app version information (e.g., iOS version, App version)

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide real-time translation services
  • Operate and secure user accounts (authentication, fraud prevention)
  • Determine subscription status and provide premium features
  • Improve the App (debugging, performance optimization)
  • Comply with legal obligations (where applicable)

3. Data Processing, Retention, and Storage

3.1 Processing

Audio and text may be temporarily transmitted to third-party speech recognition or translation services to enable core functionality. These services process data only to provide the requested feature.

3.2 Retention

  • We do not permanently store your audio recordings or translation content.
  • Account information (email, user ID) is retained while your account remains active.
  • Diagnostic logs may be retained for a limited period to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

3.3 Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data using industry-standard practices.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services to provide core features and functionality, including:

  • Cloud-based speech recognition services
  • Cloud-based translation services
  • Apple In-App Purchases (subscriptions)

Third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies as well.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share information only in the following limited cases:

  • With service providers needed to operate the App (e.g., speech/translation providers), solely to deliver requested functionality
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights and safety
  • In connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition), subject to appropriate safeguards

6. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on where our servers and service providers operate. We take steps to ensure an appropriate level of protection for such transfers where required by law.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can stop using the App at any time.
  • You can revoke microphone and speech recognition permissions in your device settings.
  • You may request account-related assistance (e.g., account deletion) by contacting us.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of any updates.
